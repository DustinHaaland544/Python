Just a quick update on the Pylint library. To see the same report that I do in the video, in the newest version of Pylint you need to add -r y(report yes) at the end of the command should 

be:

pylint myexample.py -r y

If you check out the video I only wrote pylint myexample.py, you will need to add on -r y

Check out the previously answered QA Forum posts if you get on this, thanks!
